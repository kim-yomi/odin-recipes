Pork Sinigang

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Papaya
  • Sitaw
  • Kangkong
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Begin by washing pork spare ribs in cold running water to remove any visible impurities.
  2. Then, place pork ribs submerge in a medium pot with a liter of water. Bring to a boil without covering the pot and allow the scum to float to the surface.
  3. Meticulously remove the scums from the surface of the water using a slotted spoon or a spatula.
  4. After the broth clears up, turn you heat to medium setting, then itâ€™s time toss in your onions and tomatoes to add flavor to the broth.
  5. Cover your pot and let it simmer to fully cook your meat. Use a fork to pierce the meat so that the flavors would also enter the meaty parts of the spare ribs.
  6. Start by adding the sitaw, kangkong stalks and talong into the pot and cook until these turn dark green.
  7. Add in your siling sigang, kangkong leaves and the Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc Mix. Stir and let it simmer. It should be done in about 2-3 minutes.
